What Is an Affiliate Link?

An affiliate link is a specially tracked URL that connects to a third-party product or service. When you click an affiliate link and complete a qualifying action — such as making a purchase or signing up for a service — Vibesoft may receive a commission or referral fee from that third party.

The price you pay is not affected by whether you use an affiliate link. You pay the same amount you would if you navigated to the product directly.

Where Affiliate Links May Appear

Affiliate links may appear in any application published by Vibesoft LLC, including but not limited to links to:

  • Travel booking platforms and providers

  • Products or services recommended in-app

Affiliate links are not currently present on this website (vibesoftllc.com). All links on this website are informational only.

How We Identify Affiliate Content

Where technically feasible, we label affiliate links within our applications using clear indicators such as "Affiliate Link," "Partner," or similar language near the link. We are committed to making these disclosures prominent and easy to understand.

Our Commitment to Honest Recommendations

Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial decisions. We do not feature, rank, or recommend products solely because of an affiliate arrangement. Our goal is to surface the most useful information for our users; affiliate income is incidental to that goal, not the driver of it.

We only participate in affiliate programs for products and services that we believe are genuinely useful and relevant to our users.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the FTC's Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising (16 CFR Part 255). The FTC requires that material connections between endorsers and brands be disclosed clearly and conspicuously. Our affiliate relationships constitute such a material connection.
