Privacy Policy

Effective April 30, 2026  ·  Vibesoft LLC

Vibesoft LLC ("Vibesoft," "we," "our," or "us") is a Florida limited liability company. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website at vibesoftllc.com or use any application we publish. By using our website or applications, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

On This Website

This website is a static informational site. We do not operate analytics services, tracking pixels, behavioral advertising networks, or user account systems on this site. We do not collect any personal information through web forms.

If you contact us using a "mailto:" link, your email client will open a message addressed to us. Any message you choose to send will transmit your email address and message content to us.

In Our Applications

Each application we publish may collect different types of information depending on its features. The data practices specific to each app are described in that app's supplemental privacy notice, which is accessible from its App Store or Google Play listing. Information our applications may collect includes:

  • Information you provide directly (name, email address, preferences)
  • Device identifiers and operating system information
  • In-app usage and interaction data
  • Search queries and filters you configure within the app
  • Optional account information if the app supports accounts

Third-Party Services

This website loads typefaces from Google Fonts. When the page loads, your browser sends a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address. This is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Our individual applications may integrate third-party services (such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising networks). Those services and their data practices are disclosed in each app's supplemental privacy notice.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website does not set cookies or use tracking technologies of any kind. Individual applications may use local storage, cookies, or similar mechanisms for functionality (such as saving preferences); details are covered in the applicable app-specific privacy notice.

How We Use Your Information

Information received through email contact on this website is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Information collected through our applications is used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the application's features
  • Respond to support requests
  • Deliver optional notifications where you have opted in
  • Analyze aggregate usage to improve user experience

We do not sell your personal information.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third parties that help us operate our applications, under agreements that limit their use of your data to providing those services.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, court order, or valid legal process.
  • Safety: When necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Vibesoft, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case the successor will be bound by this policy.

Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained for as long as needed to address your inquiry and as required by applicable law. Retention periods for data collected through individual applications are described in their respective privacy notices.

Children's Privacy

Our website and applications are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at admin@vibesoftllc.com and we will promptly delete it.

Your Privacy Rights

All users may contact us to ask what information we hold, request correction or deletion, or raise any privacy concern.

California residents have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information is collected about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@vibesoftllc.com. We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.
